1. Site Name

Benin City Chamber of Commerce

This name represents a local business chamber that promotes networking, business growth, and visibility for companies in Benin City, Edo State.

2. Site Purpose

The website provides information about local businesses, promotes chamber membership, displays a business directory, and allows users to apply for membership online.

3. Scenarios

  • How do I register my business as a member of the chamber?
  • Where can I find contact information for other businesses in my industry?

4. Color Scheme

#1B4965 - Header & Footer
#ff6600 - Buttons & Highlights
#F7F9FB - Background

5. Typography

Font Family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif

  • Headings: Bold Arial for structure and clarity
  • Body Text: Regular Arial for readability
  • Buttons: Bold white text for visibility
